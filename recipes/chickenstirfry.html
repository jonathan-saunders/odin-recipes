Chicken Stir Fry

Description

This Chicken Stir-Fry recipe is a quick and healthy dish featuring thinly sliced chicken breast and colorful vegetables. The chicken is marinated in soy sauce and sesame oil, then stir-fried with garlic and ginger for a fragrant flavor. The addition of fresh vegetables adds a crunch and nutritional value, making this dish a balanced meal. It's a versatile recipe that can be served over rice or noodles, perfect for a busy weeknight dinner.

Ingredients

  • Boneless chicken breast, thinly sliced
  • Assorted vegetables (bell peppers, broccoli, carrots)
  • Soy sauce
  • Garlic, minced
  • Ginger, grated
  • Vegetable oil
  • Cornstarch
  • Sesame oil
  • Green onions, chopped

Instructions

  1. In a bowl, mix chicken with soy sauce, cornstarch, and a bit of sesame oil. Let marinate for 15 minutes.
  2. Heat vegetable oil in a wok or large skillet over high heat. Add garlic and ginger, stir-frying for a minute.
  3. Add the chicken and stir-fry until it turns white and is nearly cooked through.
  4. Add the assorted vegetables and continue to stir-fry until vegetables are tender and the chicken is fully cooked.
  5. Drizzle with a bit more soy sauce and sesame oil. Garnish with green onions.
  6. Serve hot with rice or noodles.
