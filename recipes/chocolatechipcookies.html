Chocolate Chip Cookies

Description

This classic Chocolate Chip Cookies recipe is a beloved sweet treat. The cookies are made with a blend of brown and white sugar, creating a perfect balance of flavors. The addition of vanilla extract and semi-sweet chocolate chips infuses each cookie with a rich, decadent taste. The outside of the cookie is slightly crisp, while the inside remains soft and chewy. These cookies are ideal for sharing with friends and family or enjoying with a glass of milk.

Ingredients

  • All-purpose flour
  • Baking soda
  • Salt
  • Unsalted butter, softened
  • Brown sugar
  • White sugar
  • Vanilla extract
  • Eggs
  • Semi-sweet chocolate chips

Instructions

  1. Preheat oven to 350°F (175°C).
  2. In a bowl, whisk together flour, baking soda, and salt.
  3. In another bowl, cream together softened butter, brown sugar, and white sugar until smooth. Beat in the eggs one at a time, then stir in vanilla.
  4. Gradually blend in the dry ingredients. Stir in chocolate chips.
  5. Drop by large spoonfuls onto ungreased baking sheets.
  6. Bake for 8 to 10 minutes, or until edges are nicely browned.
  7. Let cool on baking sheets for a few minutes before transferring to wire racks to cool completely.
