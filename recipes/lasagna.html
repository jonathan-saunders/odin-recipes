Lasagna

Description

This lasagna recipe is a classic Italian dish that layers savory meat sauce, creamy ricotta cheese, and melted mozzarella and Parmesan cheeses between sheets of lasagna noodles. The dish is seasoned with Italian herbs for added flavor. After assembling the layers, the lasagna is baked until the cheese is bubbly and golden, creating a comforting and hearty meal perfect for family dinners or gatherings.

Ingredients

  • Lasagna noodles
  • Marinara sauce
  • Ground beef or turkey
  • Ricotta cheese
  • Mozzarella cheese
  • Parmesan cheese
  • Egg
  • Salt
  • Pepper
  • Italian seasoning

Instructions

  1. Preheat oven to 375°F (190°C).
  2. Cook the lasagna noodles as per package instructions.
  3. In a pan, cook the ground meat. Season with salt, pepper, and Italian seasoning. Add marinara sauce to the meat and simmer.
  4. In a bowl, mix ricotta cheese with an egg, some salt, and pepper.
  5. In a baking dish, layer noodles, the meat sauce, ricotta mixture, and mozzarella cheese. Repeat layers.
  6. Top with Parmesan cheese.
  7. Cover with foil and bake for 25 minutes. Remove foil and bake for another 25 minutes.
  8. Let it cool before serving.
